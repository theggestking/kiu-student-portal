About KIU

A Place Where Knowledge Creates the Future

Kutaisi International University is a modern higher education institution focused on academic excellence, innovation, and international perspectives. KIU provides students with a contemporary learning environment where education, research, and future opportunities come together.

The university is designed to support students through strong academic programs, modern infrastructure, and a growing community of learning, collaboration, and development.

Campus Life

Study, Connect, and Grow on a Modern Campus

Campus life at KIU brings together learning, collaboration, and student experience in one environment. The university campus offers modern facilities, shared spaces, and opportunities for students to study, connect, and participate in university life.

With its large campus area and student-centered atmosphere, KIU provides a place where learning continues beyond the classroom.
